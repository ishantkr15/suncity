Welcome to Sun City Public School

Established in 2006, Sun City Public School has come to symbolize quality education with the aim to provide better education & a healthy atmosphere for children. This is a co-educational school based on CBSE pattern.

The School Campus is sprawling across a large area with state-of-the-art facilities. The school's vision goes beyond the achievement of academic excellence to focus on holistic development of each student.
