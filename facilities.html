Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for holistic development

Smart Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to provide a comfortable and stimulating learning environment. Each classroom is equipped with:

  • Smart boards and digital projectors
  • Ergonomic furniture for proper posture
  • Ample natural light and ventilation
  • Interactive learning tools
  • Display boards for student work

The student-teacher ratio is maintained at 25:1 to ensure individual attention and effective learning.

Science & Computer Labs

We have well-equipped laboratories to facilitate practical learning and scientific exploration:

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern equipment
  • Computer lab with latest hardware and software
  • Mathematics lab for practical understanding of concepts
  • Language lab for improving communication skills
  • Robotics and AI lab for advanced learning

All labs are supervised by qualified instructors and maintained with strict safety protocols.

Library & Resource Center

Our extensive library houses a vast collection of resources to support learning and research:

  • Over 15,000 books covering various subjects
  • Reference section with encyclopedias and dictionaries
  • Periodicals and magazines section
  • Digital library with e-books and online resources
  • Reading room with comfortable seating

The library also hosts regular book fairs, author interactions, and reading programs to promote literacy.

Sports Facilities

We believe in the importance of physical fitness and offer excellent sports infrastructure:

  • Large playground for cricket, football, and athletics
  • Indoor sports complex for badminton, table tennis, and chess
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
  • Swimming pool with trained instructors
  • Yoga and meditation center

Our school has produced several state and national level players in various sports disciplines.

Transportation

We provide safe and reliable transportation services for our students:

  • Fleet of GPS-enabled buses with trained drivers
  • Female attendants in each bus
  • Coverage across Moradabad and nearby areas
  • Regular maintenance and safety checks
  • Emergency protocols and first-aid kits

Transport routes are optimized for efficiency and parents can track buses in real-time through our app.

School Canteen

Our hygienic canteen serves nutritious and delicious meals to students and staff:

  • Freshly prepared meals using quality ingredients
  • Varied menu with healthy options
  • Special dietary requirements accommodated
  • Regular health and hygiene inspections
  • Comfortable seating area

The canteen follows strict food safety standards and promotes healthy eating habits among students.
