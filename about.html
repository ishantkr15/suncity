Our History

Sun City Public School was established in 2006 with the aim to provide better education & a healthy atmosphere for children. Founded by a group of visionary educators, the school started with just 120 students and has grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in Moradabad.

The school began in a modest building but has since expanded to a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art facilities. From its inception, the school has been committed to academic excellence while also focusing on the holistic development of each student.
